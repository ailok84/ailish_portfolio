About Me

Graphic & Web Designer - Front End Web Developer
"I Make Beautiful Things Happen"

A Little About Me

I was born in Ireland and have been in Denmark permanently for the past two years and can speak Danish to a professional level. I have studied a MA in Visual Communication whilst living in England. I love to travel and have worked as a Graphic Designer in Australia for 3 years. I am curently updating my skills in KEA doing a MMD (Multimedia Design) AP.

I'm Really Passionate About

Design! I love Graphic Design and have developed a passion for Web Design. I am really interested in Front End Web Development and am enjoying learning more every passing week. I hope to develop my career as a Front End Web Developer. I am good at UX Design and have a good comprehension of what the user needs when using a project.

Skill Set

Graphic Design, Web Design, UX Design, Front End Development. I am a skilled user of Adobe CC.
