Alternativ KBH

Basic Video

City Walks & Meetup

This project was a group project. We created an idea that we would film a meet-up event for music lovers who like to go to alternative music venues in Copenhagen. We created a logo and website design.

We then created a story board and filmed the project at various locations around the city.

The editing was decided upon and the short clip can be viewed. The website also has a video playing in the background which i edited. The website is fully responsive. This was a fun project to make!
