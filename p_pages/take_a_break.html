Take a Break

Basic Video

Narration Without Words

This project was part of the Basic Video Module. We were assigned groups and given a couple of days to develop our story and film our topic. I created the idea which was to take a break from stress and constantly checking your mobile phone.

We decided to shoot close to KEA and all had an input on the story and script development.

The project was then edited and music added to the final result.
