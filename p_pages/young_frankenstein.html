Young Frankenstein

Basic Animation

Group Project

This project was made with a group of fellow students using SVG animations and javascript. Our task was to create an animated opening sequence for the movie "Young Frankenstein". Together we came up with the idea of how the sequence would work by drawing up storyboards.

We decided to split the group tasks so we could achieve our ambitious goals within the required deadline. I was charged with coding. As the project progressed I also helped with design and sound.

The project overall was challenging but I learned a great deal about SVGs and expanded my knowledge of Javascript.
